About Me

I have written Ruby for a web magazine application. I was also working on a project based on MongoDB and Redis to generate music magazines automatically. In the most recent project, I helped create an Android app from ground up and write the first front-end test framework for our website.

I joined this company after I graduated from Dartmouth College. I have been intern in Google (Google Search iOS App), Yelp (Android App + Python API), Microsoft (Mobile App) and another China based startup when I was in school. I am used to the startup lifestyle and love the idea of quickly iterating a product.

Uber is definitely one of the most exciting products I have heard of in recent years. Despite its exponential growth, I am impressed by its vision that building a network of drivers to deliver everything, everywhere. As an engineer, rapid growth and challenge engineering problems are the most attractive parts of a job.
